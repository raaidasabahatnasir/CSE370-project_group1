Risk Score Methodology
Pending Compliance Issues: Each overdue compliance issue adds **10 points** to the risk score.
Past Accident Records: Each recorded accident within the last 12 months adds **10 points**.
Overcrowding Incidents: Each instance of exceeding worker capacity adds **10 points**.
